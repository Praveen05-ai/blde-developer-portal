📖 Research Library & FAQs

Before requesting a consultation, explore our standard templates and research methodology documentation below.

What is a Clinical Research Blueprint?

A Blueprint (.bldebp) is a read-only clinical advisory guidelines package. It contains study design recommendations, sample size justifications, and data collection structures designed by senior consultants.

What is a Project Setup Package (.bldeproj)?

It is an encrypted configuration payload. Importing it instantly instantiates a fully validated database structure (instruments, fields, events, and validation checks) on your dashboard, with 0-manual configuration.

How do we ensure FDA & GCP compliance?

All study designs are vetted by senior biostatisticians and research consultants. Full audit trails verify that no patient clinical records are ever exported in project packages.

❓ Help & FAQs

            
Frequently asked questions and guides on managing the BLDE(DU) EDC platform.

          

        

        

          

            

              How do I register a new Project Incharge (PI) account?
              ▼
            

            

              Go to the Users module in the sidebar, click the red + Add User button, fill in the Name and Email address, and choose **Project Incharge** from the role selector. Click register to create their login. Administrators can only register Project Incharge accounts.
            

          


          

            

              How do I activate or deactivate (suspend) user accounts?
              ▼
            

            

              Open the Users page from the sidebar. In the users list table, locate the operator you wish to manage, and click the status slider button (toggle between Active and Inactive) to suspend or restore their platform access instantly.
            

          


          

            

              How do I assign Project Incharges to specific research projects?
              ▼
            

            

              Go to the **Users** module, scroll down to the **Project Permissions** section, select the desired research project from the dropdown list, click **+ Grant**, select the Project Incharge and choose their access level (Read/Write/Admin), then click submit.
            

          


          

            

              What permissions do Institutional Administrators have?
              ▼
            

            

              Administrators possess full view access across all projects, can manage and suspend users, monitor data entry progress, approve setup templates via the Research Assistant tickets queue, and export institutional reports. Admins **cannot** write participant records, design survey forms, or modify data capture rules.
            

          


          

            

              How do I view consultation blueprints from the Research Assistant?
              ▼
            

            

              Go to the **Research Assistant** page in the sidebar menu. In the active tickets queue list, click on any consultation ticket. You can review the blueprint requirements submitted by the Research Assistant, request revisions, or click **Approve Blueprint** to launch the project.
